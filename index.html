Academic journal fit analysis and proposed literature discovery in one workspace.

Journal Match Literature Scout PDF Export

Smart match resolves your APA references in OpenAlex (DOI or title), boosts journals where those papers were published, adds “neighbourhood” venues that publish work citing them, then blends OpenAlex source search and the curated catalogue for quartile / publisher / guideline detail where available. The catalogue tab searches that same OpenAlex journal index live (not Scimago SJR quartiles—verify metrics on Scimago or the journal’s site).

The app first pulls OpenAlex works that cite your seed citations, then searches OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, and Crossref in parallel (with fallbacks), and re-ranks in your browser. Google Scholar cannot be queried from the page—after results load, use the Scholar links for the same queries in Google’s UI. Internet required.
